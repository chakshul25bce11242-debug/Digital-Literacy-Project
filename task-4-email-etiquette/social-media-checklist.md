# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s
1. Use respectful and polite language while posting, commenting, or messaging.
2. Check facts before sharing any news, information, or screenshots.
3. Keep your profile professional and appropriate as a college student.
4. Protect your account with a strong password and privacy settings.
5. Think carefully before posting anything that may affect your reputation.
6. Use social media to learn, network, and stay informed in a positive way.
7. Respect other people’s opinions, privacy, and digital boundaries.

## Don’ts
1. Do not share personal information such as phone number, address, passwords, or OTPs publicly.
2. Do not post offensive, abusive, or harmful content about anyone.
3. Do not spread rumours, fake news, or unverified information.
4. Do not upload inappropriate photos, videos, or comments that may harm your image.
5. Do not engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or online arguments.
6. Do not click suspicious links or respond to unknown messages carelessly.
7. Do not share college-related content irresponsibly if it may create misunderstanding or conflict.
