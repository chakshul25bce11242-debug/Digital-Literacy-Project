## Project Overview
This repository contains my Digital Literacy Project submitted as part of the CSE0001 course. The project was designed to help students understand the importance of digital literacy in academic, personal, and professional life. Through this project, I explored different aspects of digital awareness such as creating a digital literacy infographic, building a student digital portfolio, using coding and collaboration platforms, practising professional email etiquette, and learning about cybercrime awareness.

The work is organized into five tasks, each corresponding to one module of the course. All screenshots, documents, and write-ups have been arranged in separate folders for clarity and easy access.

---

## Repository Structure

- `task-1-presentation/` – Digital Literacy infographic/poster
- `task-2-portfolio/` – Screenshots of professional/academic profiles
- `task-3-platforms/` – Coding platform activity and Google Form screenshots
- `task-4-email-etiquette/` – Professional email drafts and social media checklist

- `report/` – Final project report in PDF or DOCX format

## Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
For this task, I created an infographic on digital literacy using Canva. The poster explains the meaning of digital literacy and highlights its importance for students, including safe internet use, responsible online behaviour, and professional digital presence.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
In this task, I created and updated my profiles on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and other relevant digital platforms. This helped me understand the value of building a professional online identity from the beginning of college.

### Task 3 – Coding and Collaboration Platforms
This task involved exploring a coding practice platform and completing a beginner-level challenge. I also created a Google Form quiz on digital literacy and viewed responses through a linked Google Sheet, which helped me learn about digital collaboration tools.
**Google Form Link:** https://forms.gle/TtAVnDPB8zXzitUv9

### Task 4 – Professional Email and Etiquette Guide
For this task, I wrote two professional emails: one requesting an assignment deadline extension and another expressing interest in a summer internship opportunity. I also created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts for responsible online behaviour.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study and Prevention Guide
In this task, I prepared a case study on identity theft and created a prevention checklist for staying safe online. This helped me understand common cyber risks and the importance of protecting personal information.
